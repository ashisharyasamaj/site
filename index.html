Marriage Documents

  • Arya Samaj Marriage Documents are required which are important like as: Age Proof, Residency Proof, Passport Size Photographs, Witnesses and Signature.
  • If any party is divorced then divorced paper is must. application form duly signed by both husband and wife.
  • Documentary evidence of date of birth of parties (Matriculation Certificate / Passport / Birth Certificate)
  • Minimum Age of Boys is 21 years & Girls is 18 years at the time of registration under the Hindu Marriage Act.
  • Residence Proof of Both Parties (Voter I Card, Driving License, Ration Card, Aadhar Card, Passport, Domicile Proof, Electricity Bill, Water Bill etc.)
  • Affidavit by both the parties stating place and date of marriage, date of birth, marital status at the time of marriage and nationality.
  • Four passport size photographs of both the parties
  • 2-2 Witnesses of both parties with residential proof (Voter I Card, Driving License, Ration Card, Aadhar Card, Passport Etc.)
  • Things: 2 Jaimala (Garlands), Sweets, Sindoor, Mangalsutra and 3-5 type of Fruits.
